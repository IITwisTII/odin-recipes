Arnabeet

Description

Aleppo Poor People Dish. Also childhood dish for me and easy to make.

Ingredients

  • Minced beef
  • Onion
  • Cauliflower
  • Salt and peppar
  • Olive oil
  • Arabic bread
  • Turkish yoghurt

Steps:

  1. Oil up Cauilflowers and set in oven
  2. Fry beef with salt, peppar and Onion(?)
  3. Mix up the meat with the Cauliflowers
  4. Make yoghurt with garlic
  5. Eat with arabic bread
