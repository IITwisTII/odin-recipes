Burger

Description

Sister taught me this one. Easy to make, people love them

Ingredients

  • Burger Buns
  • Minced Beef
  • Onions
  • Tomato
  • Pickles
  • Ketchup
  • Mayo
  • Sliced cheddar cheese

Steps:

  1. Slice vegetables
  2. Fry Burger patties + place cheese to melt
  3. Design the burger however you want
  4. Enjoy!
