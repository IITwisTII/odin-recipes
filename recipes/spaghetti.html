Spaghetti

Description

This recipe is my fathers. I use it to make a quick meal prep in Uni.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti Pasta
  • Basilica sauce
  • Mushrooms
  • Paprika
  • Onions
  • Salt and peppar
  • 500g minced beef

Steps:

  1. Fry Onions
  2. Add in 500g Minced beef
  3. Mix in salt and peppar
  4. Add in basilica sauce
  5. Boil Pasta for 10 mins
  6. Add in Mushrooms and Paprika
  7. Serve!
